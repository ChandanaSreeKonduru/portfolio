Hi, I'm Chandana

AI Product Manager • Builder • Problem Solver

I bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and human-centered design. With 5+ years of experience building products that matter, I've learned that the best solutions come from deeply understanding both the technology and the people who use it.

My Journey

My path into product management wasn't traditional. I started as a software engineer, writing code and building features. But I kept finding myself asking "why" — why are we building this? Who is it for? What problem does it solve?

That curiosity led me to product management, where I discovered my true passion: translating complex technical capabilities into products that make people's lives easier.

Today, I specialize in AI-powered products. I believe artificial intelligence is most powerful when it's invisible — when it just works, making experiences smoother and more intuitive without users even noticing.
